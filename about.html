Who We Are

We’re a Ghana-based borehole drilling company focused on quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

Our Story

Iklyn Borehole Services exists to help homes, businesses, farms, and institutions access clean and reliable water. We understand local ground conditions and apply the right drilling approach for long-lasting results.

From assessment to drilling and pump installation, we work with professionalism and clear communication — so clients know what to expect at every stage.

Our Approach

  • Site assessment and practical recommendations
  • Proper drilling and casing for durability
  • Test pumping to confirm water yield
  • Clean finishing and safe installation
  • After-service support and maintenance options
